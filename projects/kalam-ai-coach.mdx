# Kalam: Your Real-Time AI Communication Coach

## Overview

Kalam is an ultra-low latency, voice-first AI coach designed to help you master job interviews, public speaking, and difficult conversations through real-time roleplay and personalized feedback. Built for the Mistral AI Worldwide Hackathon 2026!

Whether it's a high-stakes job interview or a difficult negotiation, the best way to improve is through practice. Kalam acts as your conversational sparring partner, listening naturally and responding instantly with a lifelike voice to keep you on your toes. 

Once your session ends, Kalam steps out of character to become your expert coach, providing a detailed breakdown of your performance.

## Key Features

- **Instant Voice Roleplay:** Engage in natural, flowing conversations with near-zero hesitation or delay.
- **Dynamic Personas:** Practice against unique characters tailored to your specific scenario—from strict hiring managers to casual friends.
- **Adaptive Session Timers:** Practice in focused bursts (1 to 5 minutes) with visual countdowns that help you manage your time under pressure.
- **Feedback Scorecards:** Get evaluated on clarity, confidence, and context. Download your personalized PDF scorecard to track your progress!

## How It Works

Kalam leverages the power of Next.js 16 (App Router) combined with React hooks for stateful audio streaming. The system uses Mistral Large (`mistral-large-latest`) in JSON mode to reliably generate character constraints before the session, and to output structured, multi-metric performance scorecards afterward.

To ensure real-time latency, the audio pipeline works right in the browser utilizing the Web Audio API alongside ElevenLabs' incredibly fast text-to-speech and speech-to-text systems.

## Technologies Used

- **ElevenLabs API**
  - **Speech-to-Text (STT):** ElevenLabs `scribe_v1` via Web Speech API for fast audio transcriptions.
  - **Text-to-Speech (TTS):** ElevenLabs `eleven_turbo_v2_5` with optimized streaming latency (`optimize_streaming_latency=3`) for instantaneous replies.
- **Mistral AI:** Evaluative intelligence and dynamic character persona generation using JSON Mode.
- **Frontend Framework:** Next.js 16 (App Router) & React.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4.
